Timeline

Global Hope has had a great history over the years that we are proud of, we have been helping all kinds of people internationally for a long time.

1985 - Foundation and Initial Mission

In 1985, a group of activists and professionals concerned about the growing environmental crisis and humanitarian needs founded "Global Hope" in Burgos, Spain. The organization was born with the mission of improving the quality of life of vulnerable communities and protecting the environment through sustainable projects.

1995 - Expansion to Health and Education Programs

In 1995, the NGO expanded its focus to include health and education programs. In collaboration with local governments, Global Hope began establishing mobile clinics in remote regions of India and literacy programs for women in Bangladesh, promoting gender equality and access to essential services.

2005 - International Recognitions

In 2005, Global Hope was awarded the World Sustainability Award for its efforts in promoting sustainable development and environmental protection. This international recognition boosted the NGO's visibility and increased its ability to raise funds and attract volunteers.

2015 - Response to Natural Disasters

In 2015, after several devastating natural disasters, Global Hope created a specialized emergency response team. This team provided immediate assistance to communities affected by the earthquake in Nepal and organized sustainable reconstruction efforts in the Philippines after Typhoon Haiyan.

2024 - Current Programs and Future

Today, Global Hope operates in over 40 countries, with an integrated focus on human well-being and environmental sustainability. The NGO remains committed to its mission of creating a future where all people can live in a healthy and sustainable environment, using education, technological innovation, and global collaboration to address the challenges of the 21st century.
