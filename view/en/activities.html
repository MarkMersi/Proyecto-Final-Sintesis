Activity Table

At Global Hope, we organize outdoor activities to raise funds for our projects. These activities provide great fun for families and friends, while also helping people in need.

Our different activities include:

  1. Gymkhanas and Sports Competitions:
    • Organizing themed gymkhanas for families and friends with paid registrations.
    • Football, basketball, or volleyball tournaments with local teams.
    • Sponsored obstacle races and marathons.
  2. Charity Hikes and Walks:
    • Guided hikes to natural parks and conservation areas.
    • Charity walks in iconic locations with participation fees.
    • Night walks or bike rides.
  3. Markets and Fairs:
    • Second-hand markets and garage sales.
    • Craft fairs with handmade products by volunteers and local artisans.
    • Organic and local food markets.
  4. Workshops and Seminars:
    • Workshops on crafts, cooking, gardening, and other practical skills.
    • Seminars and talks on sustainable development and humanitarian aid topics.
    • Professional training courses and workshops with registration fees.
