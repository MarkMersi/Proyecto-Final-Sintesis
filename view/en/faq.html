Support

Need help? Have a question? Read our frequently asked questions, if you have any other question not on this list, feel free to contact us using the contact form.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is "Global Hope"?

    2. "Global Hope" is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded in 1985 with the mission to improve the quality of life of vulnerable communities and protect the environment through sustainable and collaborative projects.

  2. What are the main focus areas of "Global Hope"?

    3. Our focus areas include:

    • Community development and access to clean water.
    • Community health and education.
    • Environmental conservation and reforestation.
    • Response to natural disasters and emergencies.
  3. How can I support "Global Hope"?

    4. There are several ways to support our mission:

    • Making a monetary donation through our website.
    • Participating in our fundraising events.
    • Volunteering your time and skills in our projects.
    • Sharing our cause and activities on social media.
  4. How are the received donations used?

    5. Donations are used to fund our field projects, cover essential operating costs, and support our awareness and education campaigns. We ensure that each donation has a significant and measurable impact on the communities we serve.

  5. Where does "Global Hope" operate?

    6. We operate in over 40 countries in Africa, Asia, Latin America, and the Caribbean, focusing on areas where humanitarian and environmental needs are most critical.

  6. How can I get involved as a volunteer?

    7. You can get involved as a volunteer by visiting our website and completing the registration form. We offer both local and international opportunities, tailored to different skills and time availabilities.

  7. Do you organize fundraising activities?

    8. Yes, we organize a variety of fundraising activities, such as scavenger hunts, sports competitions, excursions, charity walks, bazaars and fairs, as well as workshops and seminars.

  8. How can I register for a fundraising event?

    9. You can register for our fundraising events by visiting your local Global Hope office; on our website, you will find detailed information about the events we organize.

  9. Does "Global Hope" collaborate with other organizations?

    10. Yes, we collaborate with local governments, other NGOs, international organizations, and the private sector to maximize the impact of our projects and ensure their long-term sustainability.

  10. How can I stay up to date with "Global Hope's" activities and projects?

    11. You can subscribe to our newsletter on our website, follow us on our social networks, and visit our blog for regular updates on our activities and projects.

  11. What kind of environmental conservation projects does "Global Hope" carry out?

    12. Our environmental conservation projects include reforestation of degraded areas, biodiversity conservation, promotion of renewable energies, and education on sustainable practices.

  12. Does "Global Hope" offer job opportunities?

    13. Yes, we offer employment and internship opportunities in various areas. You can find more information about current vacancies and how to apply in the jobs section of our website.
